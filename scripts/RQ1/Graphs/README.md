# Graphs

This folder contains the figures generated for RQ1.

The figures visualize the perceived relevance of SDGs, their direct connection to SME business operations, the relationship between these two dimensions, and the largest rank differences between perceived relevance and direct business connection.

## Files

* `RQ1_01_mean_relevance_SDGs.png` – mean perceived relevance of each SDG.
* `RQ1_02_direct_connection_SDGs.png` – share of SMEs directly linking each SDG to business operations.
* `RQ1_03_rank_comparison_relevance_vs_direct.png` – comparison of SDG rankings based on perceived relevance and direct business connection.
* `RQ1_04_scatter_relevance_vs_direct.png` – relationship between perceived SDG relevance and direct business connection.
* `RQ1_05_largest_rank_gaps.png` – largest rank differences between perceived relevance and direct business connection
